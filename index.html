Hello and welcome to our website! We are four young women involved in the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program! In this course, we, as a group of four, got the opportunity to make anything we wanted using our knowledge of code that we learned over five weeks of study. We chose to apply our education to create a game and website to spread awareness about the threats that polar bears are facing due to global warming and climate change.

Check out this slideshow with a bunch of cute images of polar bears! On this website you will can find our game, details about what you can do to help, and information about us! We hope you enjoy!
