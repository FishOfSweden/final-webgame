The Facts:

~ The polar bear is an endangered species.

~ Polar bears need to eat around 12,325 calories per day according to a recent study, 60% more than what scientists had previously thought.

~ Sea ice is shrinking 14% per decade due to climate change, causing major changes in the Arctic and affects the eating patterns of polar bears.

~ There has been a decrease of 770,000 square miles since the median measurement in data from 1981-2010.

~ It is predicted that by 2050, the population of bears will be reduced to 10,000, two-thirds less than now.

~ There has been a 40% decrease in population in the last 10 years in the Beaufort Sea region.

~~~~ Information from National Geographic: https://news.nationalgeographic.com/2018/02/polar-bears-starve-melting-sea-ice-global-warming-study-beaufort-sea-environment/

~ The population in the Beaufort Sea deminished from 1,500 to 900 bears. "This is a clear warning sign of the impact a warming Arctic has on ice-dependent species like the polar bear," stated Dr. Pete Ewins, WWF Senior Species Officer in Canada.
